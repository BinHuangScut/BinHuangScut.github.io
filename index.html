Bin Huang

Ph.D. Candidate @ SMU

Email: bin@smu.edu

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering under the supervision of Dr. Jianhui Wang at Southern Methodist University, Dallas, USA.

I received the B.S. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, in 2016, and the M.S. degree in electrical engineering from South China University of Technology, China, in 2019.

My research interests include but are not limited to:

  • Machine learning, deep learning, and data analytics for power systems
  • Decision-making methods and their applications on smart grid
  • Reinforcement learning for energy management and storage
  • Probabilistic and stochastic methods in power system analysis
  • Physics-informed neural networks and their applications in power systems

I am open to research discussions and collaborations. Feel free to reach out to me via email!

Openings: I am open to motivated Ph.D. students and postdocs. Feel free to drop me an email (bin@smu.edu) with the subject "{PhD/Postdoc Application}-{Your Name}" (e.g., PhD/Postdoc Application-Ming Li) with your CV, transcripts (for Ph.D. applicants; postdocs may omit), and representative publications (if any) attached, if you are interested. Please include a brief statement of motivation for joining the research group in the email.
