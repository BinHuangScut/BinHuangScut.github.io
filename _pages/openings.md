<p><strong>I am open to motivated Ph.D. students and postdocs to work with me on topics in power systems, machine learning, and smart grid applications.</strong></p>

<div class="faculty-profile">
  <h4>How to Apply</h4>
  <p>If you are interested, please send me an email at <a href="mailto:bin@smu.edu">bin@smu.edu</a> with:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Subject line:</strong> <code>{PhD/Postdoc Application}-{Your Name}</code> (e.g., PhD/Postdoc Application-Ming Li)</li>
    <li><strong>Attachments:</strong> Your CV, transcripts (for Ph.D. applicants; postdocs may omit), and representative publications (if any)</li>
    <li><strong>In the email:</strong> A brief statement of motivation for joining the research group</li>
  </ul>
  <p>I will reply to promising candidates. For more about my work, see <a href="/Research/">Research</a> and <a href="/publications/">Publications</a>.</p>
